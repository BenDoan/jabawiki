Personal
=======

Tech
====
* What languages are used?
* Are automated tests important?
* What's the frontend/backend split?
* What OS is used?

Team
====
* Is the project interesting?
* Is the team a cost center?
* Is there on-call work?

* What's the dress code?
* How flexible are the hours?
* Can I work remotely?
